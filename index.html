About the Department

This department is responsible for planning and development of water resources, river basin management, and project implementation in India.

Water is the foundation of life on Earth. From drinking and cooking to agriculture, sanitation, and industry, every aspect of human existence depends on the availability of clean and safe water.

  • Only about 1% of Earthâ€™s water is easily accessible for human use
  • Agriculture consumes the largest share of freshwater resources
  • Millions of people face water scarcity due to pollution and misuse
  • Healthy water systems protect ecosystems and biodiversity

Responsible water usage, timely reporting of water-related issues, and sustainable management practices are essential to ensure water security for present and future generations.

This portal enables citizens to register water complaints, explore water management initiatives, and learn about rainwater harvesting and official government services.
